The Plagues

Message Notes & Discussion Guide | Exodus 4-11

📝 Record It
🗣️ Discuss It
1. When Moses first obeyed God, things actually got worse for the Israelites (they lost their straw). Has doing the right thing ever made your life harder at first? How do you keep trusting God?
2. Even Pharaoh's magicians admitted, "This is the finger of God" (Exodus 8:19). His own advisors knew they were beat! Why do you think Pharaoh still refused to listen?
3. Pharaoh tried to compromise with Moses (e.g., "Go worship, but leave your flocks behind"). What are some ways we try to "compromise" with God today by holding parts of our life back?
🚀 Do It (Weekly Action Steps)
1. Check Your Heart: Is there an area of your heart that has grown stubborn or hard toward God? (A grudge, a rule you keep breaking, a warning you are ignoring). Pray Psalm 139:23-24 and ask God to soften it.

2. No Compromise: Identify one area of your life you have left back in "Egypt" (your money, media, relationships). Make a commitment to bring 100% of your life under God's authority this week!
