This Week's Bible Study

The Plagues of Egypt | Exodus 4-11

Message Notes & Key Points

Hey Keystone Youth! This week we are diving into Exodus chapters 4 through 11. Make sure you have your Bible open as you watch the video above.

Scripture Focus: "And the Egyptians shall know that I am the Lord, when I stretch out My hand on Egypt and bring out the children of Israel from among them." - Exodus 7:5

Things to Pay Attention To:

  • God vs. The False Gods: Notice how the 10 plagues weren't just random weather events. God was systematically defeating every false god the Egyptians worshipped (the Nile, the frogs, the sun, etc.).
  • The Stubborn Heart: Pay attention to how many times Pharaoh says he will let the people go, only to change his mind as soon as the plague stops. Are there areas in our lives where we do the same thing to God?
  • No Compromise: Pharaoh tried to tell Moses, "You can go, but leave your animals behind." He wanted to keep a leash on them. God demands total surrender—no compromise!
